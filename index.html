About Me

I'm Hasibul Islam, a self-driven trader and developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My world runs on two parallel charts — candlesticks and code — and I've spent years learning to read both with discipline.

As a Binary & Forex Trader, I study price action, manage risk with strict rules, and treat every trade as a data point rather than a gamble. Above all, I'm a Problem Solver and Technology Enthusiast — I enjoy breaking complex systems into simple, repeatable processes, whether that's a trading strategy or a software architecture.
